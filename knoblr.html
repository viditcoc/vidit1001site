From Idea to production of a seamless and collaborative communnication platform.

At Knoblr we have created an innovative next generatin whiteboard collaboration & communication platform. We solved these recurring communication pain points:
• Brings every vision to life with simultaneous visual and verbal explanations - capturing and connecting Chains of Thought.
• Bridges cross-team and department communication, linking every communication session so nothing gets lost in translation and transition.
• Plays back any Huddle at specific timestamps - with synchronized voiceover and illustrations
• Shareable with team members as well as nonmembers via emailable link
•With Knoblr’s fully integrated platform, you have the option to do much more before, during and after your meetings without compromising comfortability ofthe tools you use everyday.
